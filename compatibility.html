Compatibility Checks

  • CPU + Motherboard: Socket and chipset must match.
  • RAM: DDR4 vs DDR5 must match the motherboard.
  • Case size: Make sure your motherboard form factor fits.
  • PSU wattage: Enough power for CPU/GPU with extra headroom.
  • GPU clearance: Card length must fit inside the case.
