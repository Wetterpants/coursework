Helpful PC Building Resources

Beginners and experienced builders commonly use the tools and websites below to plan PC builds, check compatibility, and research parts before buying.

Build Planning & Compatibility

  • PCPartPicker – A popular tool for selecting PC parts, checking compatibility, and estimating total cost.
  • Newegg – Useful for comparing prices, reading reviews, and checking part specifications.
  • Amazon – Helpful for customer reviews, photos, and real-world feedback on PC components.

Learning & Troubleshooting

  • r/buildapc – A community where users ask questions, get build advice, and troubleshoot issues.
  • YouTube Build Guides – Short videos can help visualize steps like installing RAM, GPUs, or applying thermal paste.

These resources are meant to support beginners while planning and building their first PC.
